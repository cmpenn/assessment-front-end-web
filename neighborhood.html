My Neighborhood!


My home is Cedar Hills Utah. A hidden gem right along the mountain side. This town has beautiful views, fun hikes, and is close to the city, so having a fine dining experience isn't to far away.

Local Hikes

  • Mount Timpanogos trail
  • Horsetail Falls
  • Lone Peak

Fine Dining

Some of this unique towns finest resturants include Betos, JCW's, and The Pie. These are only a few of the high class dining establishment around Cedar Hills Utah.
